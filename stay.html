Where to Stay in Taniti

Comfortable, safe, and welcoming lodging options

Taniti offers a wide range of lodging options, all regulated and regularly inspected to ensure safety and comfort for visitors.

Lodging Options

Resorts

Enjoy luxury accommodations with pools, dining, and ocean views.

Best for: Families & Luxury Travelers

Hotels

Comfortable hotels located near Taniti City and Merriton Landing.

Best for: First-time visitors

Bed & Breakfasts

Family-owned lodging with local charm and personal service.

Best for: Couples & Cultural Travelers

Hostels

Affordable accommodations for backpackers and solo travelers.

Best for: Budget Travelers

Popular Areas to Stay
  • Taniti City: Walkable, cultural attractions, dining
  • Merriton Landing: Entertainment, nightlife, beaches
  • Near Yellow Leaf Bay: Scenic views and water activities

Many accommodations are within walking distance of attractions or easily accessible by public transportation.

What to Expect

Safety & Regulations

All lodging on Taniti is strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the local government.

Languages

Many hotel staff speak English, especially in tourist areas.

Accessibility

Larger hotels and resorts offer accessibility-friendly rooms and facilities.
